Round Robin Simulator on Raspberry Pi

In our project, we have simulated preemptive round robin scheduling on Raspberry Pi. We basically created three process for this purpose which prints even numbers, odd numbers and prime numbers in a circular pattern. We have used the idea of polling where there was a need to handle interrupts.

Download it from here and feel free to extend it..
